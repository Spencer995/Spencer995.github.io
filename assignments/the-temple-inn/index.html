Aba, Nigeria Temple

The Aba, Nigeria Temple is the only operational temple in Nigeria. It was dedicated by President Gordon B. Hinckley on 7 August, 2005. The temple had been a long awaited blessing for Latter-Day Saints in Nigeria, and since its dedicataion, it has been a great source of light and spiritual refinement for them.
Indeed, The temple is the house of the Lord.
