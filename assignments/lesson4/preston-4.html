The Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide quick, easy and ready to use weather information to users.
The site will be designed to updated automatically, according to real time weather condition on
selected area. The site will provide weather information of major cities and sub-localities across
the world.
The site is also designed to give a live view of what a given destination looks like and the time.
