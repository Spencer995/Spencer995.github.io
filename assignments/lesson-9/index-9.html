The Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide quick, easy and ready to use weather information to users. The site will be designed to updated automatically, according to real time weather condition on selected area. The site will provide weather information of major cities and sub-localities across the world. The site is also designed to give a live view of what a given destination looks like and the time. Everybody needs to know about the weather, so why not make it available to everyone in a way so easy it seems almost seamless. That is simply the purpose of theweatherbank.com.
The site is designed to suit all ages, and people from all works of life, this is done that to allow anyone who uses or needs to use the site to easily access weather information with little or no help at all.

Use the weatherbank.com today, and love the weather like never before.
